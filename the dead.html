THE DEAD



"The Dead" is the final short story in the 1914 collection Dubliners by James Joyce. The other stories in the collection are shorter, whereas at 15,952 words, "The Dead" is almost long enough to be described as a novella. Wikipedia
Originally published: 1914
Author: James Joyce
Genre: Fiction
Original language: English
Adaptations: The Dead (1987), James Joyce's The Dead, The Last Step (2012)
